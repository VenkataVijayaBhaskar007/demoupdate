Grow Your Savings with Our Recurring Deposit

A Recurring Deposit (RD) is a special term deposit that helps you build savings through regular monthly deposits. Ideal for salaried individuals, students, or anyone looking to grow wealth in a disciplined manner, RDs offer attractive interest rates and guaranteed returns.

You invest a fixed amount every month, and at the end of the chosen tenure, you receive the total principal along with accrued interest. It's a smart way to build a savings habit and reach your financial goals.

With flexible tenure options and the benefit of compounding interest, Recurring Deposits provide a low-risk, steady savings plan with assured returns.
